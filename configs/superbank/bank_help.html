Commands

/openaccount Opens a new account.
/balance Displays the player's balance in chat.
/moneywithdrawn Shows the player how much they have withdrawn so far this round.
/maxdep Deposits all of the player's cash into their account.
/maxwit Withdraws cash until either the player has $16k, they reach their limit for that round or they have no money left in the account.
maxdep (console) Same as /maxdep.
maxwit (console) Same as /maxwit.
/withdraw <amount> Withdraws <amount> from the player's account.
/deposit <amount> Deposit <amount>.

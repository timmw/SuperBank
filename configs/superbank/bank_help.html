Commands

/openaccountOpens a new account.
/balanceDisplays the player's balance in chat.
/moneywithdrawnShows the player how much they have withdrawn so far this round.
/maxdepDeposits all of the player's cash into their account.
/maxwitWithdraws cash until either the player has $16k, they reach their limit for that round or they have no money left in the account.
maxdep (console)See /maxdep.
maxwit (console)See /maxwit.
/withdraw <amount>Withdraws <amount> from the player's account.
/deposit <amount>Deposit <amount>.
